Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate mountain lovers, Astourist - Depot began as a small initiative to share the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas with fellow adventurers. What started as weekend trips to Shimla and Manali has evolved into a comprehensive travel service provider.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted travel partners in Himachal Pradesh, having served over 50,000 happy travelers from across the globe. Our deep local knowledge, experienced guides, and commitment to sustainable tourism set us apart.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking and traveling with us.

Our packages typically include accommodation, meals (breakfast and dinner), transportation, sightseeing activities, and professional guide services. Specific inclusions vary by package, so please check the detailed itinerary for each tour.

You can book online through our website by selecting your preferred package, filling in traveler details, and completing the payment. We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking. You'll receive instant confirmation via email.

Cancellations made 30 days before departure receive a full refund minus processing fees. 15-30 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: No refund. However, we understand emergencies happen and try to be flexible whenever possible.
